Cheasecake

This red fruit cheesecake recipe is simply delicious! The combination of the sweetness of the strawberry and the slightly acidic flavor of the blueberry creates a fresh and balanced dessert. Plus, the creamy texture of the cheesecake contrasts perfectly with the crunch of the biscuit base. If you are looking for an easy-to-make dessert that will impress your guests, this is the ideal recipe.

Method of preparation

Ingredients:

  • 100g of butter
  • 300g cream cheese
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 1 tablespoon colorless gelatin powder
  • 1 cup chopped strawberries
  • 1 cup of blueberries

Steps:

  1. Grind the cookies and mix with the melted butter. Forge a shape and take it to the fridge.
  2. Beat the cream cheese, condensed milk and lemon juice. Add dissolved gelatin.
  3. Pour over the biscuit base and refrigerate for 2 hours.
  4. Top with fruit and serve chilled.
